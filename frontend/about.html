About the project

Welcome to FRoot, a linguistic resource developed as part of a programming course at the University of Zurich. Dive into our user-friendly French language exploration tool, where you can discover the historical roots of modern French words, access in-depth etymology and definitions, and ensure precision in your language use. Join us on a captivating linguistic journey through the world of French words.

Purpose

The purpose of our application is to provide users with a user-friendly interface that enables them to access and interact with a large language data set effectively. This interface is designed to facilitate various tasks, including querying the data set, linking different aspects of it, and creating an enjoyable and visually pleasing user experience.

For our target users, having some prior knowledge of the French language is beneficial. Specifically, those interested in exploring the Old French roots of modern French words will find our tool particularly useful.

Our application aims to enhance users' understanding of words by offering comprehensive information. This includes providing etymology, definitions, and lemma forms for both the user-input word and its perceived meaning. By conducting this dual analysis, our tool assists in reconciling any discrepancies between the user's intended meaning and the standard dictionary definition, thereby ensuring accurate language usage.
